What types of applications does VelocityQA test?
We specialise in testing applications for healthcare, education, retail, and ecommerce startups. We test across iOS, Android and web platforms.
How much does QA testing cost?
Our packages start at ($565) for Bug Hunter. We also offer User Journey Testing ($1,125), System Auditor ($1,560), and hourly testing at $125/hour.
Do you require long-term contracts?
No. We operate on a pay-as-you-go model with 14-day terms.
How quickly can you test my application?
Same-day testing is available. Packages range from 24-36 hour turnaround.
Do you service all of Australia?
Yes. We're based in Brisbane but serve startups and businesses nationwide.
How quickly can you start testing my app?
We can typically begin testing within 24 hours after getting access and a brief on your requirements.
What information do you need to provide a quote?
We need app type, complexity, testing goals, timeline, and concerns. A 15-min call helps us quote precisely.
Do I need to provide test cases or test plans?
Not required. We create strategies based on your goals. Existing documentation is welcome but optional.
What kind of reporting do you provide?
We provide clear reports with reproduction steps, visuals, and prioritised issues based on impact.
How does your blocked hours option work?
Purchase hours in any amount at $125/hour or 10 hour blocks at $100/hour. Use them for smoke tests, pre-release checks, or ongoing testing as needed.
