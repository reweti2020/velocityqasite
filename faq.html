Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our services. If you don't find your answer, please reach out to us.

How quickly can you start testing my app?

We can typically begin testing within 24 hours of receiving access to your application and a brief on your testing requirements. Our streamlined process eliminates the lengthy setup times common with larger QA firms.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we need to know the type of application, its complexity, specific areas you want tested, your timeline, and any particular concerns. We can often give ballpark estimates based on our package options, but a brief 15-minute call typically allows us to provide a precise quote.

Do I need to provide test cases or test plans?

No, we'll develop our own testing strategy based on your requirements. While we welcome any existing test documentation you have, it's not required. Our approach focuses on real-world usage patterns and exploring edge cases beyond what typical test plans would cover.

What kind of reporting do you provide?

Our reports are clear, concise, and actionable. Each issue is documented with steps to reproduce, screenshots or videos. We prioritise issues based on severity and user impact, allowing your team to focus on what matters most.

Do you test on real devices or emulators?

We primarily test on real devices to ensure accurate results. Our testing includes iOS, Android and mac. However, we also leverage emulators for preliminary testing, refer to emulator checklist.

How does your blocked hours option work?

Our blocked hours option gives you maximum flexibility. You can purchase testing time in hourly increments with no minimum, at a rate of $100 per hour. This is ideal for quick smoke tests, last-minute pre-release validation, or ongoing testing throughout your development cycle. Hours can be used as needed, and we'll provide detailed reporting on how the time was utilized.
