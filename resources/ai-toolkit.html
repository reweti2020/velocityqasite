COMPETITOR IDENTIFICATION - Direct competitors - Indirect competitors - Potential new entrants - Substitutes 4. COMPETITOR PROFILES (FOR EACH KEY COMPETITOR) - Company background and overview - Financial performance and resources - Market share and positioning - Target customer segments - Value proposition and messaging 5. PRODUCT/SERVICE COMPARISON - Feature and functionality comparison - Pricing strategy analysis - Quality and performance benchmarking - Innovation and R&D capabilities - Product roadmap (if available) 6. MARKETING AND SALES STRATEGIES - Brand positioning and awareness - Marketing channels and tactics - Sales approach and distribution - Customer acquisition strategies - Customer retention and loyalty programs 7. CUSTOMER ANALYSIS - Customer perception and satisfaction - Switching costs and loyalty factors - Unmet customer needs - Voice of customer insights 8. SWOT ANALYSIS - Strengths relative to competitors - Weaknesses to address - Market opportunities to pursue - Competitive threats to mitigate 9. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS - Competitive positioning opportunities - Differentiation strategies - Defensive tactics - Growth and expansion opportunities - Potential partnerships or acquisitions 10. IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP - Short-term actions (0-6 months) - Medium-term initiatives (6-18 months) - Long-term strategic moves (18+ months) - Resource requirements - Success metrics and KPIs
