App Explorer: Quick Issue Detection

Our App Explorer service is designed for startups that need fast feedback on their application. In just 24 hours, we'll perform a comprehensive assessment of your app, focusing on identifying critical issues that could impact your users' experience.

This service is perfect for:

  • Pre-launch validation to catch major issues before release
  • Sprint-end reviews to identify problems before demo
  • Feature testing to validate new functionality
  • Initial assessment before more in-depth testing

We recently helped a fintech startup identify a critical data validation issue just hours before their beta launch. The problem would have allowed users to submit invalid payment details, potentially resulting in failed transactions and user frustration.

Crash Detector: Stability Assurance

Our Crash Detector service is a deep dive into the stability of your application across different devices, operating systems, and usage patterns. We methodically push your app to its limits to identify crash scenarios before your users encounter them.

Key aspects of this service include:

  • Comprehensive testing across multiple device models
  • OS version compatibility testing
  • Memory and resource load testing
  • Network condition simulation (poor connectivity, sudden disconnection)
  • Background/foreground transition testing

For a health tracking app, our Crash Detector service identified a critical issue that only occurred on Android 11 devices when users attempted to sync their data while the app was transitioning from background to foregroundâ€”an edge case that would have affected approximately 18% of their user base.

Flow Finder: User Journey Optimization

Our Flow Finder service focuses on the complete user journey through your application. We test all critical user paths to identify workflow issues, logic gaps, and usability problems that could create friction or dead-ends for your users.

This comprehensive service includes:

  • Mapping and validation of all key user journeys
  • Testing of multiple flow variations and alternative paths
  • Edge case and boundary testing
  • Identification of inconsistencies and logic errors
  • Workflow optimization recommendations

For an e-commerce startup, our Flow Finder service uncovered a critical disconnect in their checkout process that was causing 23% of users to abandon their carts. By identifying and fixing this issue, they saw a 15% increase in conversion rate in the first week after implementing our recommended fixes.

What You'll Receive

Our testing services are designed to provide clear, actionable information that helps you improve your application.

Detailed Bug Reports

Every issue we find is documented with clear steps to reproduce, environment information, and severity classification. We include screenshots and videos where relevant to help your team understand exactly what's happening.

Prioritized Issue List

We categorize issues by severity and user impact, giving you a clear roadmap for what needs to be fixed first. This helps your development team focus on the most critical problems that could affect user experience or retention.

Fix Recommendations

Unlike many testing services that simply point out problems, we provide actionable suggestions for resolving each issue. Our team's experience across hundreds of apps gives us insight into common solutions that can save your developers time.

Digital Assets

You'll receive all screen recordings, screenshots, and logs collected during testing. These are valuable not only for resolving current issues but also as reference material for future development and quality assurance efforts.

Performance Metrics

For applicable packages, we provide key performance metrics such as load times, response times, and resource usage. These insights help you identify potential bottlenecks that could affect your app's performance under real-world conditions.
