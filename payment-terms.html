Payment Terms

Clear, simple terms that work with your startup’s pace.

Standard QA Packages

All VelocityQA packages require a 50% deposit at the time of contract agreement. The remaining 50% is due within 14 days after the project is completed.

Precision Strike Packages

Our intensive bug-finding “Precision Strike” packages must be paid in full upfront at the time of contract agreement.

Late Payment Policy

To keep our workflow efficient and client-focused, overdue payments may incur a 5% late fee for every 7 days past due. Please reach out if you foresee any issues—we’re always happy to work with you.
