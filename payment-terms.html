Payment Terms

Simple and transparent payment expectations for every project.

Standard QA Packages

All VelocityQA packages require a 50% deposit when the contract is signed. The remaining 50% must be paid within 14 days of project completion.

Precision Strike Packages

Our high-impact “Precision Strike” testing options require full upfront payment at the time of agreement, due to their rapid delivery and limited availability.

Late Payment Policy

If final payment is not received within the 14-day window, a 5% late fee will apply every 7 days overdue. Please contact us in advance if there are any concerns—we’re happy to work with your schedule.
