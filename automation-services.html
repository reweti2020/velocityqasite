Frequently Asked Questions

How is your automation service different from others?

Unlike traditional services that require complex infrastructure or ongoing contracts, we deliver ready-to-use scripts that your developers can run immediately. Our approach focuses on practical, maintainable automation that fits your existing workflow rather than replacing it.

Do I need to know how to code to use these automation scripts?

No coding knowledge is required. We provide clear documentation on how to run the scripts, and both Maestro Studio and Katalon have user-friendly interfaces. Your developers will find it easy to import and execute the tests even without prior automation experience.

How long does the implementation process take?

Our Rapid Smoke Test package delivers scripts in just 3 days, while the Core Workflow package takes 5 days. This is significantly faster than the industry standard of 2-3 weeks for similar services. We achieve this through our focused approach and specialized expertise with Maestro and Katalon.

What happens if my application changes after you deliver the scripts?

We provide 10 days of post-delivery support to address any immediate issues. For longer-term maintenance, our Quarterly Maintenance Package ensures your scripts stay up-to-date with application changes. This service includes regular reviews and updates to accommodate UI changes and new features.

How do we measure the 40% reduction in regression testing time?

This metric is based on real results from our clients, comparing their manual testing time before and after automation implementation. For example, we've helped mobile app clients reduce their regression testing cycle from 5 days to 3 days by automating repetitive test cases, allowing their QA resources to focus on exploratory testing and new features.
