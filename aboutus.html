Our Story

Putting Quality Within Reach of Every Startup

VelocityQA was born after being approached by friends and colleagues to perform testing on their startup apps and websites. It quickly became obvious there was a real gap for startups and small to medium business owners trying to juggle resources and complete proper QA testing..

While working at enterprise-level corporations, we witnessed how quality assurance was often a luxury that only well-funded companies could afford. Startups were forced to choose between expensive QA agencies, hiring full-time specialists they couldn't justify, or simply releasing untested products that damaged their reputation.

Our approach is different. We focus on brutal testing - using apps and sites as actual users would. We don't just follow the "happy path" that works every time; we actively look for bugs, customer pain points, irritations, and broken elements. These are precisely the issues that will drive customers away to find another solution.

What sets us apart is that our time is spent on testing, not creating extensive plans, workflows, or reports - which can be a time sink when you're resource and time poor. You need to know what's wrong and how to fix it quickly. As a startup founder, you're likely performing many roles, and QA is often last on the list but can be most critical to successfully launching your site or app.

We understand the importance of being first to market, the time constraints of small teams, and the need for developers to know what's wrong quickly so they can fix and turn it around. It can make all the difference.
