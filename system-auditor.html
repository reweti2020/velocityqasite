Target Testing for Mid-range Applications

Our System Auditor package is designed for web applications, admin dashboards, and systems with numerous user pathways. We strategically test key aspects of your application to identify issues that could impact user experience, data flow, and core system performance.

Our experienced testers follow a structured approach that includes validation of primary navigation systems, essential component consistency checking, data entry, and workflows.

This service is ideal for:

  • Recipe management platforms
  • Cafe and small business POS systems
  • Small to medium business dashboards
  • E-commerce or web application user journeys
  • Pre-launch validation of complex systems
  • Regression testing for existing apps after significant updates

Our report will provide a list of key issues found, full defect list, representative screenshots, critical workflow recordings, and clear reproduction steps to help your development team efficiently address the most important problems.
