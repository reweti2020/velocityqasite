Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How quickly can you start testing my app?

We can typically begin testing within 24 hours of receiving access to your application and a brief on your testing requirements.

Do you test on real devices or emulators?

We primarily test on real devices to ensure accurate results. Our testing includes iOS, Android and Mac. However, we also leverage emulators for preliminary testing.

How does your blocked hours option work?

Our blocked hours option gives you maximum flexibility. You can purchase testing time in 10 hourly blocks, to be used whenever you want until you use the hours.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

We need app type and access details (URL, TestFlight link, or App Store link), specific areas of concern, and your testing goals. It's also helpful to know your timeline expectations, which platforms and devices are priorities, any user roles that need testing, and budget constraints if applicable. A brief 15-minute discovery call often helps us understand your needs more thoroughly and provide a precise, tailored quote for your project.
