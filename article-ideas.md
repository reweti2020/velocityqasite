# VelocityQA Article Ideas

This document contains planned article ideas focused on Startup QA pain points, particularly for non-technical founders and early-stage startups.

## Article Ideas

1. **The Non-Technical Founder's QA Toolkit**
   - Translation guide for QA terminology
   - Visual flowcharts for testing processes
   - Decision trees for quality issues
   - Target: Founders without technical backgrounds

2. **DIY Testing: MVP Quality on a Bootstrap Budget**
   - Practical testing strategies when you can't afford dedicated QA
   - Prioritization templates
   - Free/low-cost tools guide
   - Critical vs. nice-to-have testing areas

3. **User Feedback Pipeline**
   - System for converting user comments into actionable bug fixes
   - Feedback capture templates
   - Categorization framework
   - Prioritization matrix for user-reported issues

4. **The Hidden Gaps: Launch Checklist for Non-Technical Founders**
   - Critical testing areas most frequently missed
   - Pre-launch verification steps
   - Founder-friendly launch sequence
   - Common post-launch emergency fixes to avoid

5. **Outsourcing Red Flags**
   - Quality warning signs when working with development agencies
   - How to address quality concerns professionally
   - Questions to ask potential development partners
   - Verification steps for delivered work

6. **Technical Debt in Business Terms**
   - Explaining the real costs of skipping QA
   - Financial impact assessment worksheet
   - Technical debt warning signs
   - Business case for investing in quality

7. **Quality Metrics for Business Founders**
   - Simple measurements that matter for product quality
   - Non-technical KPIs for app performance
   - Dashboard examples for tracking quality
   - When to worry vs. when to celebrate

8. **Progressive QA Framework**
   - How to gradually implement testing as you scale
   - Stage-appropriate testing from founding to Series A
   - Resource allocation guide by company stage
   - When to hire QA specialists vs. using external resources

9. **Production Crisis Playbook**
   - Step-by-step emergency response plan
   - Communication templates for users/stakeholders
   - Technical diagnosis questions for developers
   - Post-mortem process to prevent recurrence

10. **Developer Diplomacy**
    - Communication templates for quality discussions
    - How to discuss bugs without creating defensiveness
    - Questions that get results from technical teams
    - Building quality culture when you're not technical

11. **First-Time Mobile App Testing Guide**
    - Specific checklist for entrepreneurs launching mobile applications
    - Device testing strategy on limited resources
    - App store submission gotchas
    - User acceptance testing for mobile interfaces

12. **Practical UX Testing for Non-Designers**
    - Simple techniques to evaluate user experience
    - Guerrilla testing methods
    - Interpreting user behavior without UX expertise
    - When to fix UX vs. when to redesign
